General Information

Bins are collected EARLY on Monday mornings. We put the bins out on Sunday night. The alternations of the bins are:
18th Jan - GREEN BIN
25th Jan - YELLOW
1st Feb - GREEN
11th Feb - YELLOW
Please there is NO NEED to mow the yard as that is going above and beyond and will cut into important golf playing time
Pool is cleaned by a company on or about the 17th of each month. They take care of everything however the pool may need topping up with water every now and then. Usually fill to two tiles from the top is sufficient.
The pool is heated automatically and should not need any adjusting.
The outdoor area has an automatic shutting roof when rain is falling it wwill close. You can adjust it with the remote next to the door. Select zone 1 or 2, open and close as desired.
Plants we usually give a water each afternoon. Dont worry about the planters on the side near the ktichen as they felt the wrath of Stella and she pulled them out - there is literally zero left to water... The chillis are very edible - however warning are hot. Thyme and oregano in the vegie pod are also free for use.
